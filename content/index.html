Features

Repetition dectector

Keep your code DRY and you'll not need to fix the same bug twice. SidekickJS's repetition detection is smart enough to see even structual repetition - regardless of the words or formatting.

Complexity analysis

It's sometimes hard to know when your code is complex - metrics flag any areas that are becoming a problem.

JSHint

The baseline: keep a house style, and spot potential dangers before they cause bugs.

TODO tracking

TODOs and FIXMEs are broken windows - don't let your codebase rot. Track, priroties and fix.
