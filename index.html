What does SidekickJS do for you?

Your team loves a challenge: keep the bar high

Great teams produce great work. Show you're dedicated to writing great code, and your team will rise to the challenge.

No hurt egos in code-review

Code-review can feel personal if you pick up on the same issues again and again. Sidekick automatically annotates the easy stuff so you can concentrate on mentoring.

Your boss will let you refactor

Your boss can have a hard time seeing why you should spend time on existing code. Tracking technical debt provides accountability, and gets you your cleanup sprint.

Features

Repetition detection

Keep your code DRY and you'll not need to fix the same bug twice. SidekickJS's repetition detection is smart enough to see even structural repetition - regardless of the words or formatting.

Complexity analysis

It's sometimes hard to know when your code is complex - metrics flag any areas that are becoming a problem.

JSHint

The baseline: keep a house style, and spot potential dangers before they cause bugs.

TODO tracking

TODOs and FIXMEs are broken windows - don't let your codebase rot. Track, prioritise and fix.
